High Caliber

A board game where players can take coins, buy bullets, and shoot others. Each shot has a probability of hitting based on bullets owned. Players scan unique characters for abilities, and the gun is an app that requires physical gyroscope-based shooting.

Team Size: 7 | Duration: 3 weeks

My Role: Game mechanics and systems design, playtesting, prototyping, research, planning and team coordination

Driving Simulation

A small simulation where I explored the creation of different vehicle physics, Researched the city of Cascais using Google Maps and Google Earth, then designed a track layout based on it. I also created different vehicles, all of which were available for the player to edit live in the game in levels 2,3,4. I also explored rain and particle systems and made the vehicles behave differently in different conditions. For the purposes of this prototype I didn’t do any UI/UX during the creation of this project

Slimy Escape

A game developed with the GXP Engine, where players control a slime navigating through challenging levels filled with obstacles. To reach the end of each level, players must cleverly interact with objects in the environment, such as boxes, teleporters, and levers, in order to open doors ,and make use of available paths.

Team Size: 7 | Duration: 2 weeks

My Role: Game mechanics, level design, enviroment implementation, prototyping and testing, planning and team coordination

Celestial Clash

The objective was to develop a game using the GXP Engine that could also be played with a custom-built controller designed by our team. We created a boxing game where players dodge enemy attacks by lifting their feet off pressure plates and strike during the correct timing windows, using a separate single button controller to allow the players to punch they opponents.

Team Size: 6 | Duration: 2 weeks

My Role: Game mechanics, prototyping and testing, creation and implementation of controllers, research and analysis
