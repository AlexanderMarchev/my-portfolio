My Journey

At first I wasn’t sure where my passions would lead me, so I studied at the Fourth Foreign Language School Frederic Joliot Curie, a language-focused school where I learned French extensively.

My interest in technology and interactive media has been with me since childhood, but I didn’t always know where it would take me. During those early years, I was still exploring my interests and figuring out what direction I wanted to follow.

As I continued to grow, I realized that my passion for computers, creativity, and systems thinking pointed me toward game development, especially the technical and design aspects behind how games work and feel.

That realization led me to Saxion University of Applied Sciences, where I’m now studying game design with a personal focus on simulation systems and user-centered experiences. My journey has taught me the value of exploring different paths, Iterating and testing before settling on a final decision.
